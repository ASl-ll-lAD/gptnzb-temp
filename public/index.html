About

Established in 1959, Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad stands as one of Telangana's top-tier diploma institutions, consistently ranked among the state's best. Affiliated to the TGSBTET, and approved by AICTE, We are dedicated to shaping motivated technocrats equipped to contribute to national development.

GPTNZB offers a robust lineup of full-time, three-year diploma programs across disciplines like Electrical & Electronics, Computer Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics & Communication, Civil Engineering, and Chemical Engineering (Sugar Technology). Admissions are streamlined via TS POLYCET, while annual tuition remains affordable, making it accessible to a wide range of learners. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, complemented by frequent assessments and continuous skill-building. Nestled in a serene, green campus, GPTNZB combines traditional charm with modern facilities like A well-stocked central library with approximately 16k books and around 18 journals, Two separate hostels for boys and girls, with capacities of ~100 and ~90 beds respectively. These accommodations feature furnished rooms, separate washrooms, study desks & daily upkeep, Well-equipped departmental labs, workshops, a cafeteria, sports fields and gym, auditorium.

GPTNZB's vibrant student community enjoys a rich blend of academic rigor and cultural engagement. The campus buzzes with sports events, cultural fests, technical gatherings, and literary and dramatic showcases—fostering holistic personal development in a clean and supportive environment.

Overall, Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad is more than just an institution—it's a nurturing ground where future technocrats are empowered through technology, guided by committed faculty, and supported by well-rounded infrastructure. Whether you're seeking practical learning, affordable access, or a launch pad for technical careers, GPTNZB offers a compelling journey.

History

Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, was established with the vision of providing accessible and quality technical education to students from diverse backgrounds. Over the years, it has grown into a reputable institution known for producing skilled diploma graduates who are industry-ready.

Admissions

Admissions to Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, are conducted through the state-level polytechnic entrance examination. Students are selected based on merit, ensuring that only deserving candidates gain entry into diploma programs.

The admission process generally involves:

  1. Qualifying in the State Board of Technical Education entrance test.
  2. Participating in centralized counseling for seat allotment.
  3. Submission of required documents and verification by the authorities.

Reservation policies are followed as per government regulations, ensuring inclusivity and opportunities for disadvantaged groups. Detailed guidelines are announced every academic year by the State Board of Technical Education and Training.
