Courses

Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, offers three-year diploma programs across multiple disciplines:

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering – Machine design, thermal sciences, and manufacturing processes.
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering – Structural analysis, building technology, surveying, and construction management.
  • Diploma in Electrical & Electronics Engineering – Electrical circuits, power electronics, and renewable energy systems.
  • Diploma in Computer Engineering – Programming, software development, database systems, and networking.
  • Diploma in Electronics & Communication Engineering – Digital electronics, embedded systems, and communication technology.

Courses are structured into six semesters, balancing theory with practical labs, workshops, and industry-relevant projects. Continuous evaluation ensures students graduate with both knowledge and professional skills.
