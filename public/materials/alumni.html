Alumni

The alumni of Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, form a strong network spread across industries, government services, and academia. Many graduates have secured positions in public sector undertakings, multinational companies, and higher education institutions, contributing significantly to technological advancement and social development.

The alumni association serves as a bridge between past and present students, organizing reunions, mentorship programs, and knowledge-sharing sessions. Alumni also extend financial and technical support for scholarships, infrastructure improvements, and student-led initiatives.

Their success stories serve as an inspiration for current students and reinforce the legacy of the institution.
