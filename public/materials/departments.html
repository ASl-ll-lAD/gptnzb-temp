Departments

The college houses several specialized departments, each providing focused instruction in its respective field:

  • Mechanical Engineering – Design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and workshop practices.
  • Civil Engineering – Surveying, construction technology, environmental engineering, and structural design.
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering – Power systems, electrical machines, and industrial automation.
  • Computer Engineering – Programming, networking, web technologies, AI, and data analytics.
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering – Communication systems, microprocessors, and digital circuits.

Each department is supported by qualified faculty, specialized laboratories, and collaborative projects. Together, they foster an environment of innovation and problem-solving.
