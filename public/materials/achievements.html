Achievements

Over the decades, Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, has earned a reputation for academic excellence and innovation. Students have won recognition at district, state, and national levels in technical competitions, project exhibitions, and hackathons.

  • High placement rates in core engineering companies and IT firms.
  • Alumni who have advanced to top universities and research institutions.
  • Faculty-led projects published in journals and presented at national seminars.
  • Institution recognized for implementing skill development programs aligned with government initiatives.

These achievements reflect the institution's commitment to quality education and its role in shaping future-ready professionals.
