Events

Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, organizes a wide range of academic, cultural, and technical events throughout the year. These events encourage holistic development and provide platforms for students to showcase their talents.

  • Technical Fests – Events featuring project exhibitions, paper presentations, coding contests, and robotics challenges.
  • Cultural Programs – Annual day celebrations, music, dance, drama, and art competitions to foster creativity.
  • Sports Meets – Inter-collegiate and intra-collegiate tournaments promoting teamwork and fitness.
  • Workshops & Seminars – Hands-on training in emerging technologies, career guidance sessions, and motivational talks.

These events not only add vibrancy to campus life but also build confidence, leadership, and teamwork among students.
