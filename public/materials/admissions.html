Admissions

Admissions to Government Polytechnic, Nizamabad, are conducted through the state-level polytechnic entrance examination. Students are selected based on merit, ensuring that only deserving candidates gain entry into diploma programs.

The admission process generally involves:

  1. Qualifying in the State Board of Technical Education entrance test.
  2. Participating in centralized counseling for seat allotment.
  3. Submission of required documents and verification by the authorities.

Reservation policies are followed as per government regulations, ensuring inclusivity and opportunities for disadvantaged groups. Detailed guidelines are announced every academic year by the State Board of Technical Education and Training.
