Curriculum

The curriculum is designed to balance academic rigor with practical training. Each program is spread across six semesters:

  • Year 1 – Fundamentals of sciences, mathematics, engineering graphics, and workshop practices.
  • Years 2 & 3 – Domain-specific subjects supported by laboratory work, projects, and case studies.

Mandatory internships, industrial visits, and final-year capstone projects strengthen industry exposure. The curriculum also integrates soft skills, environmental studies, and entrepreneurship development, preparing students for lifelong learning and adaptability.
